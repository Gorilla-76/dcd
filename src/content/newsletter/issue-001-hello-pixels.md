Welcome to the first issue of the **DCD Dispatch** — the newsletter from the Digital Creative Department.

We are the team behind the screens. Web, brand, motion, product design, the occasional interactive thing that surprises everyone (including us). This dispatch is the public-facing breadcrumb trail of what we're shipping, breaking, and learning.

## What lives in here

- **Shipped.** Things that went live in the last two weeks. With links.
- **In flight.** What's on the bench. What's likely to land next.
- **Found.** One link, one tool, one weird thing we want the rest of the company to see.

## Why this exists

Most of our work happens in browser tabs nobody else has open. The dispatch is the receipt. Read it, ignore it, archive it — but at least now you can't say you didn't know.

— DCD
